YouTube Fundraising Plan

Chosen category: Vlogs

Target audience: Gen Z’s & Millennials

The primary purpose of understanding what your audience’s interests are is to know what type of content to display...

To be more effective, we need to know what they're interested in or what they enjoy based on their age and gender; like daily vlogs, travelling, and many more. Creating these types of vlogs can boost the audience's interest...

Audiences like recommendations; giving their experience or feedback about life and trips...

Audiences are interested in videos that are edited excellently because good editing makes the video more exciting and easy to watch...

The target audience wants to watch exciting places and activities and needs to be entertained and interacted by the vlogger...
